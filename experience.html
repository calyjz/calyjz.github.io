Experience

Undergraduate Research Assistant

In Fall 2023, I participated in the UR2PhD Program hosted by the Computing Research Association and their Undergraduate Research Methods course, where I developed foundational research skills. I also had the opportunity to work with graduate student Megan Sumner and Dr. Matthew Guzdial at the University of Alberta to develop a project called Mechanic Maker. Mechanic Maker is a level design tool that employs an assistive AI agent that aims to make game development more accessible to users without prior programming knowledge. I mainly worked on improving the current rule-based model's accuracy and user interaction with the AI agent. More on my groups work can be found here.
